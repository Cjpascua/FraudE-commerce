About Us

We are made up of small groups of retailers who aim to sell original thrift clothes on a reasonable price. We aim to provide the younger generations a feeling of what the trend in clothing that was booming back in the older generations. It all started with a group of 5 people who loves streetwear. Each clothing are handpicked and restored in a way that it is like the time it was new. at fist, we were hesitant to buy things that we dont have certainty to prove that will give us a profit but with a little bit of risk, we sucessfully became famous and made a website.
